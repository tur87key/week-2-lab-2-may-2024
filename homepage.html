Rug Tufting

Rug tufting is a craft where you use a specialized tool called a "tufting gun" to punch loops of yarn through a stretched fabric, creating a custom rug by essentially "stitching" a design with yarn onto a fabric base, allowing for intricate patterns and textures; it's a faster and more accessible way to make rugs compared to traditional hand-knotting methods.
